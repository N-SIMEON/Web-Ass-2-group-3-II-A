# Stakeholders — AgriStack

## Primary Stakeholders

### 1. Farmers / Cooperatives (Farmers)
- **Who:** Individual smallholder farmers and registered cooperatives (e.g., Kinigi Potato Coop)
- **Role:** Post harvest listings with quantity, price, and pickup location
- **Pain Points:** Price manipulation, no direct buyer access, harvest waste
- **Goals:** Fair prices, direct sales, income predictability
- **Tech Level:** Basic smartphone + internet access
- **Primary Actions:** Create listings, track approvals, receive booking notifications

### 2. Aggregators / Bulk Buyers (Buyers)
- **Who:** Urban wholesalers, supermarket procurement staff, hotel/restaurant purchasing managers, school canteen suppliers
- **Role:** Browse approved listings and submit bulk booking requests
- **Pain Points:** Inconsistent supply, multiple middlemen, difficulty verifying quality/quantity
- **Goals:** Reliable bulk supply, direct pricing, traceability
- **Tech Level:** Comfortable with web applications
- **Primary Actions:** Browse market, place orders, track order status, confirm collection

### 3. Platform Admin
- **Who:** AgriStack operations team / district agricultural officer
- **Role:** Approve/reject listings, approve orders, monitor all transactions, maintain audit log
- **Pain Points:** Manual record keeping, lack of oversight on transactions
- **Goals:** Platform integrity, transparent operation, dispute resolution
- **Tech Level:** Professional
- **Primary Actions:** Review listings, approve orders, monitor dashboard stats, view audit log

---

## Secondary Stakeholders

### 4. Rwanda Agriculture Board (RAB)
- Interest: Improved market access for smallholders, food security data
- Engagement: May provide data partnerships, certification support

### 5. Musanze District Local Government
- Interest: Economic development in agriculture sector
- Engagement: May mandate use of platform for registered cooperatives

### 6. Banks / MFIs (e.g., BPR Rwanda)
- Interest: Financing agricultural supply chain
- Engagement: Could use platform transaction history for loan assessment

### 7. Logistics Partners
- Interest: Transportation contracts for bulk potato movement
- Engagement: Could integrate pickup/delivery coordination
